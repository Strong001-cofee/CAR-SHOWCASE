Lamborghini Aventador SVJ

Manufacturer: Lamborghini

Year: 2018

Price: ₹ 7,41,25,600

The Lamborghini Aventador SVJ is a high-performance supercar with a naturally aspirated V12 engine.

It boasts 770 horsepower and can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.8 seconds.

The SVJ stands for "Super Veloce Jota," indicating its track-focused and high-performance nature.

Porsche 911 Carrera S

Manufacturer: Porsche

Year: 2023

Price: ₹ 2,10,00,000

The Porsche 911 Carrera S is a high-performance sports car known for its iconic design and rear-engine layout.

It features a 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged flat-six engine producing around 443 horsepower and 0 to 60 mph in 3.5 seconds.

The 911 Carrera S offers a blend of classic styling with modern technology and luxury.

BMW M8 Competition

Manufacturer: BMW

Year: 2023

Price: ₹ 2,44,00,000

The BMW M8 Competition is a high-performance luxury coupe with a powerful 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 engine.

It delivers 617 horsepower and can sprint from 0 to 60 mph in around 3.0 seconds.

Its aggressive design includes distinctive M styling cues and a sporty interior with advanced technology.

Audi RS 7

Manufacturer: Audi

Year: 2014

Price: ₹ 2,24,00,000

The Audi RS 7 is a high-performance luxury sedan with a sleek, sporty design. It is powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine,

delivering 591 horsepower and can sprint from 0 to 60 mph in around 3.5 seconds.
